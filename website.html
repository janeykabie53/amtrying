NAIROBI

Capital city of kenya

About

Nairobi is kenya's capital city. In addition to its urban core,the city has Nairobi National Park, a large game reserve known for breeding endangered black rhinos and home to giraffes,zebras and lions. Next to it is a well-regarded elephant orphanage operated by David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust. Nairobi is aso often used as a jumping-off point for safari trips elsewhere in kenya
